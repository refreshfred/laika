Welcome to Laika!

Laika is a "meta" demo, so to speak. It was developed in the space of two hours (design and code) by Ryan McGrath, as the basis for a presentation on getting started with Django for the Refresh Fred meetup on November 11, 2009. These slides are best viewed in a modern browser (Firefox 3.5+, Webkit, or Opera 10), but they should gracefully degrade if you're in the mood to commit a sin or two.

The following slides show the basics of Django by building out a simple slides application. They also discuss some history and general Django knowledge, so it's worth reading through from beginning to end. The source code is all available on GitHub - you *are* on GitHub, right? ;)
